Disease Prediction Based on Symptom EMR

Introduction

Digitizing medical records is a growing trend. Analyzing electronic medical records (EMR) is both a challenge and an opportunity for hospitals today.

This tool not only supports EMR analysis but also predicts potential diseases based on blood tests, medical images like CT scans, X-rays, etc.

This project builds an LSTM neural network model, one of the most significant developments in artificial intelligence, to automatically classify patient medical records to assist doctors in diagnosing potential diseases during treatment.
